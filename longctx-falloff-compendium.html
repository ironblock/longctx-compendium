Method & sources

  • Prefill curves: B70 (llama.cpp SYCL, PMZFX/intel-arc-pro-b70-benchmarks) and AMD R9700 (ggml discussion #19890, Q4_K_XL, Q4_K_M) are the best-measured at ≥4 context points. RTX 5090 MoE (hardware-corner.net, Q4_K_XL) provides 4 points. Mac M3 Ultra dense (mlx discussions #3209, mlx_lm.benchmark) provides 3 points. DGX Spark (DandinPower/llama.cpp_bench; NVIDIA Dev Forums) and Strix Halo (Level1Techs; strix-halo-guide) have 2 anchors each; remainder extrapolated.
  • RTX PRO 6000: pp512 7,640 and tg 251.6 from llm-tracker.info (Q4_K_M, 30B-A3B). NVFP4+MTP dual-card figures from loftllc.dev (~190 t/s tg) and theogravity/dual-rtx-6000-blackwell-qwen3.6-27b-fp8. Dense proxy from vaditaslim.com (Qwen2.5-32B Q4_K_M). JarvisLabs confirms 1.73× MTP gain on dense. vLLM issue #27649 warns of 55s TTFT footgun (misconfiguration; fix = correct BF16 flags).
  • DGX Spark: gb10 community confirms 273 GB/s shared SoC bandwidth; dense 32B decode confirmed bandwidth-wall-limited at ~10.7 t/s (theoretical ~15). "~1 PFLOP" is sparsity figure (~500 effective dense TFLOPS). Qwen3.5-35B-A3B TTFT/tg from NVIDIA Dev Forums (claytantor, Apr 2026).
  • Strix Halo: Use Vulkan/RADV, not ROCm (ROCm hangs on Qwen3.5 load_tensors). Set HSA_OVERRIDE_GFX_VERSION=11.5.1. Level1Techs/lhl thread and strix-halo-guide are primary sources. DeltaNet hybrid attention keeps long-context KV growth nearly flat: 4K→32K adds only 560 MB KV, tg drops 53.7→49.9 t/s.
  • Multi-GPU 3090: 2-way NVLink gives ~48% TP throughput boost over PCIe-only; 4-way (two bridged pairs over PCIe) only ~8–10% and can lower single-request decode (71→53 t/s on QwQ-32B). dzombak.com: 2× 3090 TP-2 runs Qwen3.6-27B AWQ at ~100 t/s full 256K ctx. Medium/fzbcwvv: 1× 3090 + MTP on Qwen3.6-27B reaches 85–106 t/s at 230 W cap.
  • TTFT and wall-clock: TTFT = context_length ÷ pp(context_length). Wall-clock for 16K+2K turn = TTFT@16K + (2048 ÷ tg). These are cold (un-cached) monolithic prefills; prefix caching significantly improves repeated-system-prompt agentic loops.
  • AMD R9700 included as bonus: Strong Qwen3.5-35B-A3B MoE numbers, R9700 single-card, Q4_K_XL (ggml #19890). Not in the user's original list but data quality warranted inclusion.
